# Entry 1 | Ransomware Incident at a Hospital

## Date
6/28/2025

## Description
A hospital experienced a ransomware attack after an employee opened a phishing email. This entry explains how the attack happened, what triggered it, and the first steps taken to address the issue.

## The 5 W's

### Who caused the incident?
A hospital employee, most likely a nurse or staff member with access to a workstation, unintentionally triggered the ransomware by interacting with a phishing email.

### What happened?
The employee clicked a link inside a phishing email. That link most likely executed a JavaScript based ransomware payload that encrypted important hospital files and programs.
